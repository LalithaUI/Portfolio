Design and Development

Design is an iterative process. It takes multiple revisions to learn what works and what doesn't. The purpose of design is to facilitate communication between user and content.

Designing for the web means design that suit the way people actually use the web, not how we think they should. This section looks at the discipline of web design.

Concept

We first see to understand and conceptualize. we need to understand your product, your company, your customers and your needs. Only through understanding we can give you suggestions and solutions.

Information Architecture

We work with you and gather information and define a scope for the project. We provide you with graphic design, user interface design and a full prototype development. A good Information Architecture helps us to focus on what's most important to you.

If you have an existing product/design/application we do a full evaluation in terms of W3C Standards, Usability Standards and SEO compatability. This helps us in future steps in designing and refining your product/design/application.

Design & Feedback

We follow this model as it makes sense to begin with the most visible aspect of your application/design and work through the designing and refining screen elements. This process is repeated several times to ensure that the design meets the needs of your customers.

Delivery

After the design and feedback phase our development team ensures that we have provided all the low level graphic and design elements. Our Designers provide the development team with all the .html, css, forms, templates and related material needed to build the application.

After we complete our designs, we check to ensure that the vision of the design has carried through or not. This reevaluation is a checkpoint to ensure your interface is easy to use.
