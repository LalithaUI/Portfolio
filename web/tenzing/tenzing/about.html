About Tenzing - Overview

Founded in 2003, Tenzing Solutions is a Memphis based IT solutions company. Tenzing provides end-to-end IT and business process services to clients worldwide, utilizing a highly customized, cost efficient delivery model that combines on-site and off-site operations. An organizations portfolio of applications is among its most valuable assets. Each application within the portfolio represents a targeted investment with its own challenges, objectives, cost of ownership and expected rate of return. Tenzing brings strategic objectivity in evaluating, transforming and managing your application portfolio to achieve competitive advantage and the highest rate of return.

OUR MISSION

The mission of Tenzing is to help its clients with professional services of outstanding quality, competence and objectivity, delivering the best solutions to fully satisfy client objectives in information technology, business processes and management. In all we do, we foster a culture of partnership, intrapreneurship and integrity, building a world class IT and business process outsourcing (BPO) company.

OUR VISION

Tenzing's vision is to be a world-class IT and BPO leader helping clients win and grow.

OUR VALUES

Sharing the same values allows us to enjoy considerable autonomy and swiftness of action without compromising our cohesiveness. It also allows us to mobilize teams more rapidly and bring together the most experienced individuals from across the company, who are able to quickly work as one to address a given challenge. And, of course, these values also guide our decisions and actions.
