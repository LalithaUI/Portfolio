Supply Chain / Logistics services

Information Technology is central to solving some of the most pressingchallenges in the 3PL business, including exceptions handling, supply chain integration and optimization and increased transparency in the flow of goods and information. Tenzing has extensive experience delivering IT Solutions to 3PL companies and had experience implementing and supporting solutions in the areas of

  • Transportation Management Systems
  • Supply Chain Integration Systems
  • Warehouse Management Systems
  • Back office Logistics services
Transportation Management Systems

Tenzing resources have experience implementing and Supporting TMS like OTM and I2 and have also helped clients build and implement custom TMS solutions. Tenzing resources have helped clients in the following areas

  • Requirements’ Gathering, Gap analysis and get the functionalities/workflows clarified and configure and setup the client’s environment
  • Understand and align with the client’s existing business processes, perform test cases
  • Configuration and Administration.
  • Data Management (XML,CSV) and Integration (EBS, JD Edwards,....).
  • Reports.
  • All transport Modes Setup.
  • Post-Implementation Support for Live/Production issues.
  • Help clients with carrier on-boarding during the implementation process
Supply Chain Integration Systems

Tenzing resources have experience with Sterling Integrator 5.0, GIS 4.3 &Gentran NT and have experience in the following areas

  • Installation, Administration & Maintenance(patch upgrades & performance tuning) in both Unix and windows platform
  • Administration of Gentran/GIS partner profiles, process controls, business process and experienced in handling production Issues.
  • managing the trading partner implementation process
  • Map development and testing and good exposure to the GIS services and adapters and Bpml(business process modeling language).
  • Good understanding of positional-EDI, EDI -positional, EDI-EDI ,EDI-XML and XML-EDI file translation.
  • Good exposure to ANSI X12, EDIFACT, Tradacom, RosettaNet and XML/ARIBA EDI standards
  • Knowledge of communication tools like VAN/AS2/SFTP/FTP (Connect Direct).
  • Extensive experience in implementing EDI X12 transactions like 850/855/830/860/810/940/945/943/852/856/944/204/210/211/214/867/820/846/867/852 and 990
  • EDIFACT transactions like: DELFOR/DELJIT/ INVOIC /ORDERS/ORDCHG /ORDRSP /DESADV etc.
  • Developed maps for both inbound/outbound interfaces.
