Support Services

DBA Services

Well-versed in DB2, Oracle and Microsoft SQL Server, our engineers will monitor and review daily database processes, provide performance tuning, system database administration and enhanced database monitoring services. Improve your database performance, lower your operational costs and keep your databases running efficiently and effectively with database management from Tenzing Solutions.

Our managed database services include:

  • Monitoring utilization and specified metrics determined by type of database
  • Alert notifications
  • Minimized downtime
  • Replication and database backups
  • Trouble-shooting and resolution support

Managed database services from our certified engineers will ensure that the databases you rely heavily on will continue to be up and running so you can focus on other business strategies and growth.
